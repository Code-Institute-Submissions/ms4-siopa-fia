Our Story

The story of siopaFIA began with a small shop in the small village of Glasthule in County Dublin, on Ireland's sunny east coast. siopaFia was a new kind of ethical clothing and lifestyle store, offering its customers a unique range of products that were kind and conscious towards the planet and its people. The Irish Times soon named it Ireland’s best boutique.

The same values inform siopaFIA's online store and our curated collection of ethical and sustainable fashion, skincare, footwear and more. It’s why we source all our products from makers and suppliers who share our vision that fashion shouldn’t just be about looking good. It should be good for the people who make it. And good for the planet too.

Browse our store and you will find products made from raw materials that are either recycled or sourced from environmentally-friendly ecological agriculture and organic farming. Everything is humanely produced and many of our clothes feature natural and bio-degradable dyes. We have plenty of vegan options too. Just as important, the skilled workers who make our products are paid a decent wage. Whatever you buy from us will also be packed and shipped using recyclable materials. Last but not least, we believe that being sustainable and conscious of what you buy shouldn’t mean sacrificing value, quality or your individual style. So all of the items in our range are well-priced, well-designed and made to last. That’s why we think you’ll not only look good wearing our clothes. You’ll feel good too. We hope you enjoy our online store. If you are in the area, we’d also love you to pop in, say hello and see our lovely shop.
